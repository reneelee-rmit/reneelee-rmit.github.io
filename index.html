Project Idea - Premium Petals

What is Premium Petals?

Premium Petals is a courier network app designed specifically for florists. The app will allow florists to hire verified vehicles and couriers to find localised, regular, delivery work. It will include options for tracking, proof of delivery and delivery estimates to both customers and businesses. Couriers will be inducted and trained on how to properly deliver are handle flowers and vehicles will be verified for air-conditioning and safe floral transport equipment. Having a network set up in a local area will allow for more efficient and reliable deliveries with drivers able to earn more with shorter travel time between deliveries. Couriers will be able to set their delivery range and time frame depending on the day. Premium Petal will also be able to operate as a third-party service to Shopify, a site that is widely used in the industry. This makes it easy for businesses to fulfill orders and push all existing information on orders to the couriers.

Motivation

Many businesses in the floristry industry rely on outsourced couriers to deliver their products. Although there are many delivery services out there such as Uber Delivery and Sherpa, there is a lack of specialised services available. The care required to deliver such delicate products is often neglected by these companies and flowers get damaged during transport. Premium Petals will supply the industry with couriers they can trust to deliver flowers safely and efficiently all at an affordable price. The ability to coordinate deliveries within a smaller location and have a smaller number of couriers to deliver is beneficial for businesses, couriers and customers. It allows for the couriers to execute more deliveries within a smaller about of time meaning lower fuel costs and more earning capacity. It also means that businesses are able to have their products delivered faster to their customers, ensuring a fresher product.

Features and Services

Reliable Couriers

Premium Petals offers businesses with a reliable selection of couriers who are trained in how to safely and securely delivery their flowers. Vehicles will be verified on having the appropriate equipment and utilities to be delivering flowers. Features such as air-conditioning, ample space and secure transport (eg. Vase holders, access to watering cans etc.) are essential to the safe arrival of flowers and businesses can be assured that their products will reach customers as intended.

Localised Network

Premium Petals allows couriers to set and alter their delivery zone, connecting them with florists within a local area. Having access to a network of businesses within a set zone allows for less time on the road, minimising fuel consumption and maximising earning capacity for couriers. It also provides businesses and customers with faster, cheaper and more efficient deliveries, keeping flowers fresh and maintaining quality.

Link to Shopify

Premium Petals can be linked to business’ Shopify stores, making the entire process a lot more streamlined and easier to navigate. Linking to Shopify means that orders from an online store can be transferred into Premium Petals along with all existing information including addresses and phone contact numbers of customers.

Premium Petals provides tracking and routes and navigation for drivers.

Tracking and Logistics

Premium Petals provides tracking and routes and navigation for drivers. Once a driver has accepted deliveries the app selects the most efficient route and will then navigate for them once they have left the store. Live tracking information will be available to customers and businesses. The app will also have a proof of delivery feature where couriers can upload a photo on arrival to prove that they have successfully delivered the flowers.

Tools and Technologies

The software needed to execute the project would include google maps to navigate and route deliveries, a CSS framework build and design the application and a chosen database solution to manage and store the information required. In terms of hardware this application will be done in a virtual cloud based setting. This will lower costs, ensure flexibility and efficiency for collaborating.

Skills Required

Premium Petals will require a programmer to develop the app in a language such as Python or C++ and a web designer to design the pages. These kinds of skills are both very feasible to find in the industry, there are millions of people worldwide who can program in these languages.

Outcome

If Personal Petals proves to be a successful project, florists will have access to a delivery network that is tailored to their type of business, ensuring secure and reliable deliveries. Drivers will also be able to earn more per hour and minimize fuel costs and customers will receive faster, fresher flowers.
